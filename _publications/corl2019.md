We propose MAME, a meta-RL framework with decoupled exploration and exploitation policies and self-supervised adaptation, outperforming prior baselines on continuous control benchmarks. The work was also presented as an oral at the ICML 2019 Workshop.

The paper is available at <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.13031">https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.13031</a>.
