We propose TrafficGraphNet, a GNN-based interaction prediction model for actor behavior forecasting that represents traffic actors and elements (traffic lights, stop signs) as a hybrid graph. The model explicitly captures discrete interaction modes and predicts accurate multi-agent trajectories by leveraging the structure of traffic scenes.

The paper was presented at IROS 2021 and is available at <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.13512">https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.13512</a>.
