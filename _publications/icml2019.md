We develop a GNN-based multi-agent RL model using a shared agent-entity graph for cooperative behavior, achieving state-of-the-art results with strong zero-shot generalization across team sizes. The work was also presented as a spotlight at the ICML 2019 Workshop.

The paper is available at <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.01202">https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.01202</a>.
